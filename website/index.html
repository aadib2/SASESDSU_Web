WELCOME TO THE SDSU SASE CHAPTER!

The mission of the SDSU Chapter of the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers is to create a supportive environment for our APIDA STEM student body by providing support academically, professionally, and culturally and to spotlight APIDA success in both the science and engineering industries!

About SASE

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) was founded in November 2007 to help Asian heritage scientific and engineering professionals achieve their full potential. They offer professional, collegiate, and sponsorship opportunities throughout North America.

Core Values

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

We aim to offer workshops, both technical and career-related and have guest speakers from various industries in STEM

CULTURAL HERITAGE AND DIVERSITY

We truly aim to promote and celebrate the various cultures of our members through providing a welcoming space where everyone is valued.

NETWORKING AND COLLABORATION

Through our events, we will offer a multitude of opportunities to network with API professionals in STEM and other students.

LEADERSHIP AND MENTORSHIP

We hope to foster a sense of leadership and mentorship for our members to push them to take on leadership roles and support one another.

COLLABORATION AND OUTREACH
